RE-volv helps community serving nonprofits go solar through the power of crowdfunding. We train volunteers that are passionate about clean energy to successfully run a solar crowdfunding campaign for your nonprofit.

Nonprofits save money on their electricity costs while paying RE-volv back through a lease agreement. Those lease payments are reinvested in a revolving fund, the Solar Seed Fund, which continually finances more nonprofit solar projects.

This unique model brings solar to nonprofits with barriers to financing, trains the next generation of climate leaders and supports future nonprofits going solar.

Requirments

  • We work with community-serving nonprofits interested in going solar.
  • Nonprofit preferably owns the building (but not a requirement).
  • Roof must be in good shape and less than 10 years old.

How it works

  • 1 RE-volv reviews your application and provides a quote.
  • 2 If approved, we enter into a partnership agreement.
  • 3 A RE-volv Solar Ambassador will crowdfund tax-deductible donations from people around the world through re-volv.org to finance the cost of the system.
  • 4 An accredited solar installer installs your system.
  • 5 Save 15% or more on electricity costs month after month.
  • 6 Over a 20-year lease agreement, the nonprofit makes monthly lease payments to RE-volv.
  • 7 After 20 years, ownership transfers to the nonprofit and energy savings increases.
  • 8 Lease payments are continuously reinvested into the Solar Seed Fund – a revolving fund used to finance future nonprofit solar projects. Each nonprofit helps 2-3 other nonprofits to go solar through our pay-it-forward model.
