RE-volv raises money to put solar panels on community-serving nonprofit organizations like homeless shelters, schools, community centers, and houses of worship. As these organizations pay RE-volv back, we reinvest the money into more solar projects in low-income communities across the country. It's a self-perpetuating, pay-it-forward model for solar energy that builds communities and makes them stronger.

Since its founding, RE-volv has crowdfunded solar projects for seventeen nonprofits, raising more than $1,000,000 from over 2,000 people in 38 states and 22 countries.
